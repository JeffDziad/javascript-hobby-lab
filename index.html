Introduction

Ever since the Shuttle program, I have been infatuated with the idea of exploring the vastness of space. In these seemingly futuristic times , it is more important than ever to understand why we should explore space. In these past few years, we've made amazing strides in our development of new rockets to send spacecraft and humans to space. One of the major leaders being SpaceX, who have managed to successfully land reusable rockets back on land multiple times.

Did you know?

When launching a Falcon Heavy payload to space, three boosters need a place to land. SpaceX has developed a system so precise, it can land multiple boosters within a couple hundred meters of each other.
